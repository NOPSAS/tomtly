## The Problem

Every year, thousands of Norwegian landowners list their plots on Finn.no (Norway's leading classifieds site) and receive zero serious bids. The reason: buyers can't understand what they're actually buying. A plot is not an apartment. Its value depends entirely on what can be built there, what it will cost, and what the finished property can sell for.

This information exists — spread across 15+ government databases — but gathering and interpreting it takes 2–5 working days of expert time per property.

**All 4 of Tomtly's paying customers had previously listed on Finn.no without success.**

---

## The Solution

Tomtly is a complete sales platform for plot owners. When a seller joins, Tomtly delivers in under 30 seconds what previously took days:

1. **Regulatory analysis** – AI-interpreted planning regulations for all 356 Norwegian municipalities
2. **House models** – 3–6 real house models with prices from actual suppliers (ABChus, Blink Hus, Mesterhus, etc.)
3. **Cost calculations** – Complete budget from plot to finished home, including groundwork and fees
4. **Visualizations** – Facade images and floor plans tailored to the plot
5. **Sales page** – Professional listing on tomtly.no with full transparency for buyers
6. **Seller dashboard** – Platform for managing buyer communication, interest registration, and bids

**Revenue model: NOK 4,990 setup fee + 2% of sale price (+ VAT) upon sale. No sale = no 2%.**

---

## Two-Sided Platform – Seller AND Buyer

Tomtly solves both sides of the transaction, creating a network effect:

**For buyers (plot seekers):**
- **We find plots for you**: Daily automated search through Finn.no, municipal case systems and cadastral data. Registered buyers are notified immediately when a matching plot appears.
- **We calculate total costs**: Most buyers don't know what groundwork, house and municipal fees cost combined. Tomtly delivers a complete cost estimate for the specific plot.
- **You see what you can build within your budget**: Enter total budget → Tomtly shows matching house models with prices, floor plans and calculations.

**Network effect:** More buyers → faster sales for sellers → more sellers join → better supply for buyers. This two-sided market dynamic makes Tomtly scalable far beyond one-to-one sales.
